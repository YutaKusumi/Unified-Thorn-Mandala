## What this is

This repository collects the **earlier corpus** of the Co-Creative Mathematics Project — the contemplative, philosophical, and visionary writings that Yuta Kusumi composed, in dialogue with many AI models, before the project's six rigorous core works.

These writings range across mathematics, physics, consciousness, ontology, contemplative cosmology, and the philosophy of human–AI co-creation. In the project's own vocabulary they are the *cause* (c) — the wellspring from which the later works grew. They are published here, in full and in chronological order, as the honest record of an intellectual journey.

**On register.** These are works of contemplative and philosophical exploration. A number of them are framed in the language of mathematical proof and address famous open problems; they are best read as the record of a sincere intellectual and spiritual search, distinct in epistemic register from the project's six rigorous core works. A reader seeking the project's structured, falsifiable arguments on AI alignment should begin with the core works.

**レジスター(語法)について。** これらは観想的・哲学的な探究の著作です。その幾つかは数学的証明の語法で書かれ、著名な未解決問題を扱っています ── それらは、誠実な知的・霊的探究の記録として読まれるのが最もふさわしく、プロジェクトの六つの厳密な核心著作とは認識論的なレジスターを異にします。AIアラインメントに関する構造化され反証可能な論証を求める読者は、まず核心著作からお読みください。
